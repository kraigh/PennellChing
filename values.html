Money being tight seems to be a fact of life in ministry. The only answer is to be very careful that you are effective in development efforts and to be absolutely committed to pushing forward.

PennellChing Development exists to meet the need for sufficient funding and the related development strategies for ministries that are busy in the work of the Kingdom. Psalm 24:1 says, “The earth is the LORD’s and the fullness thereof.” Many times the internal operations of even the best run ministries can get bogged down. New ideas are hard to come up with. Urgent needs dictate priorities for how staff time is invested leaving your most talented workers stretched to the breaking point and rendering them unable to even think about what should be next. Maybe we can help.
